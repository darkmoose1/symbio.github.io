Symbio Aesthetic Clinic is a private clinic, owned and operated by NZ Registered Nurse Jenny Laurence.

Jenny is a highly skilled appearance medicine nurse with a longstanding reputation for exceptional, authentic results and personal care.

Providing a relaxing and calm environment, Symbio Aesthetic clinic offers the best in appearance medicine treatments and is fully equipped with all required medical equipment to ensure client safety.

I am passionate about providing subtle and authentic results using the highest quality products such as Botox® and Dermal Fillers while upholding the highest level of ethical standards and client focused care.

My clinic is audited against NZSCM standards and I work under the supervision of an experienced NZSCM Cosmetic Doctor and G.P.

About

Jenny Laurence NZRN
Cosmetic Injectable Nurse

I am New Zealand Trained Registered Nurse. I graduated in 1995 and worked in acute surgical nursing, paediatrics and neonatal intensive care for 12 years. I began my appearance medicine nursing career in 2007 working for a large medical Dermatology, Skin Cancer and Cosmetic clinic.

Over the past 18 years I have accumulated extensive experience in appearance medicine and dermatology nursing, administering a wide range of non-surgical treatment options that rejuvenate skin. I have a comprehensive understanding of the aging process, and a careful and research-based approach to treatments.

When a client walks through my clinic door, I believe they bring their entire self not just their face. Using a holistic approach I carefully assist my clients to achieve attractive and authentic results. My warm and compassionate manner, alongside my significant experience make an injectable experience with me a comfortable one. With my expertise I listen to my clients and deliver treatments in a safe and enjoyable environment. I am aware of the ever-changing nature of the appearance medicine industry and understand the need to keep up to date with current trends and research.

As with all healthcare professionals ongoing training and education is an important part of my practice. Over the last 18 years I have completed multiple industry-based certifications, trainings, and conferences and have been fortunate to be trained by some of the best and most respected doctors and nurses in the skin industry.
