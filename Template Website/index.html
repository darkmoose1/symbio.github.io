What is Botox®?

BOTOX® is a prescription medicine obtained from the bacterium Clostridium botulinum. It is a natural protein that relaxes muscles in the treatment area leaving the skin smoother and lines minimised.

I am trained and experienced in Botox treatments of the upper and lower face. With correct doses placed precisely the results can be stunning and so satisfying.

Symbio® Bio re-modelling

Profhilo® *

What is Profhilo® ?

Profhilo® is a revolutionary treatment consisting of ultra-pure, highly concentrated Hyaluronic Acid (HA) which is injected beneath the skin.

It is made out of high and low molecular weight HA that have been thermally stabilised without the addition of any chemical cross-linking agents (BDDE chains) making it pure and naturally compatible with our skin. This results in a unique hyaluronic acid injectable gel, which has a prolonged stimulating activity on the dermal cells. When injected into skin, Profhilo® stimulates the skin cell receptors to counteract skin laxity and improve and restore firmness of the skin.

Profhilo® not only boosts and hydrates the skin, but also remodels ageing and sagging tissue. The most common areas of treatment include the face and neck and can also be used for the décolletage, hands, arms, knees and abdomen area where it can effectively treat skin laxity.

Symbio® Bio re-modelling - More information

Our skin naturally contains collagen, elastin and Hyaluronic Acid (HA). HA is the component responsible for retaining moisture and hydrating the skin, which is why it is a common ingredient in serums, moisturisers and eye drops.

Skin cells constantly renew and make these substances to maintain the structure of our skin. As we age, it takes longer for us to form these components in the skin, leading to sagging, crepey skin and fine lines.

Profhilo® acts in two ways. Once injected, it spreads evenly like runny honey under the skin, immediately hydrating the skin. As it integrates into the skin, it stimulates the production of collagen and elastin over a period of 4-6 weeks.

The effect is immediate hydration and glow of skin, followed by long-term lifting of saggy skin, reduction of crepey and fine lines, and significant improvement in skin texture and quality.

We can treat any area where there is lax, sagging or wrinkled skin. This is typically the face, neck, decolletage, and back of the hands. For the body, inner arms, inner thighs or just above the knee treatments are popular.

Anyone above the age of 30 is eligible for treatment, male or female, and can be used as a preventative treatment once a year before fine lines and sagging forms.

Treatment involves placing tiny injections of the product in specific points (10 points in total) just beneath the skin, using very fine needles. This will feel a little stingy but is usually well tolerated by most people. Topical anaesthetic cream can be offered prior to take the edge of the stinginess. The injection points will be slighty raised (bumpy) after the injections, this often resolves within 30 min, up to 24 hours at the very most.

Your first appointment takes 45 min and includes an initial consultation.

The actual treatment time is 10-15 min.

Subsequent appointments will take 20-30 minutes to allow time for follow up.

If you require local anaesthetic cream, this will take 20-30 mminutesin to be effective. Please advise me when you arrive so that we can apply the cream as early as possible.

Our treatment protocol is as follows :

  • Face and neck - two treatments spaced 4-6 weeks apart. A third treatment at 3 or 6 months post the first treatment may be required for some people depending on the condition of your skin.
  • Decolletage and hands - three treatments are recommended 4-6 weeks apart.
  • Arms, Legs, Abdomen - two treatments, 4-6 weeks apart.

Within a week you will notice that your skin feels much more hydrated. You may find that you need less moisturiser compared to normal.

You will start seeing the best effects of the treatment after THREE months from the first treatment.

Treatment effects last 6-9 months.

We recommend annual treatments. Some people may choose to do a top-up treatment every 6 months.

Profhilo® is very different from traditional dermal fillers.

It is a highly concentrated Hyaluronic Acid (HA) product (34mg/ml), free of additives, and has the added benefit being a bio-remodelling agent that stimulates collagen and elastin production.

It is injected just underneath the skin, and is therefore much safer and associated with less side effects compared to traditional fillers.

It does not volumise the face, like certain dermal fillers. It does not work on muscles unlike other treatments (but these treatments work very well in combination).

Yes. You can have other traditional filler treatments at the same time, though we prefer that these treatments are done in a separate session.

IPL, chemical peels, microdermabrasion should be done two weeks before or after Profhilo® treatment; 4 weeks for Radiofrequency or fractional resurfacing treatments.

Profhilo® is highly biocompatible with our skin, as it is made from ultra-pure Hyaluronic Acid, a substance which is naturally found in our skin, is stabilised without adding chemical cross-linking agents (BDDE chains) and is free of additives. It is therefore very well tolerated, and allergy is rare.

Since its introduction in Italy in 2015 by IBSA, a highly reputable Swiss pharmaceutical company, Profhilo® has been used extensively in Europe and worldwide for the past 7 years. With over a million treatments now administered, there has been no serious adverse effects reported.

After your treatment, you may notice localised, site-specific symptoms which are often short-lasting. This includes swelling, redness, bruising, tenderness, itching, all of which should resolve in 24 - 72 hours.

Other rare side effects can include :

  • Infection - antibiotics may be required.
  • Hard lumps - can be dissolved with Hyalase.
  • Allergy - my clinic is well equipped to deal with any reactions.
  • Vascular Occlusion - this is rare as Profhilo® is injected just underneath the skin. As with any filler, if the product inadvertently enters a blood vessel, there is a risk of skin damage, scarring, blindness or stroke. This is treated by dissolving the filler with Hyalase as soon as the complication is noted.

Covid-19 Vaccinations - we recommend that Profhilo® treatment should be done two weeks before or after your vaccination.

Covid-19 illness - we recommend that this is done 6 weeks after you recover.

Symbio® sweat reduction

Hyperhidrosis

What is hyperhidrosis ?

Hyperhidrosis is the name given to excessive and uncontrollable sweating.

Axillary hyperhidrosis

  • Clothing becomes damp, stained and must be changed several times a day.
  • Wet skin folds are prone to chafing, irritant dermatitis and infection.
  • Botulinum toxin injections are approved for hyperhidrosis affecting the armpits.
  • The injections reduce or stop sweating for approximately six months and may last longer with repeat treatments.
